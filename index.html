Skill Vistaar

Skill Vistaar addresses student confusion in career decision-making by using AI to analyze interests, skills, and goals, providing personalized career recommendations, skill gap analysis, and salary insights through a unified web platform.

Problem Statement

Students and early-career learners often struggle to choose suitable career paths due to limited understanding of industry requirements, relevant skills, and realistic salary expectations. Existing guidance methods are generic, theory-driven, and disconnected from real job-market trends, leading to uninformed decisions and employability gaps.

Literature Review / Market Study

Platforms such as LinkedIn, Glassdoor, and Indeed provide networking, job listings, or salary data individually, but do not offer an integrated, personalized career guidance solution.

Research Gap

No existing platform combines personalized career guidance, skill gap analysis, salary forecasting, and accessibility into a single, low-cost system.

System Methodology

Input Data

User form inputs, text data, and real-time API data accessed via Gemini/OpenAI-compatible models.

Architecture

Flask-based backend processes user inputs, constructs structured prompts, and retrieves AI-generated responses through OpenRouter-hosted LLMs.

Results & Analysis

Time Reduction ≈ 55%

Skill Vistaar reduces the time required for initial career guidance by automating analysis through AI. The system delivers structured recommendations within seconds, compared to manual research that typically takes several minutes.
